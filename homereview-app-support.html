About HomeReview

HomeReview helps users explore neighborhood and residence reviews before making housing decisions. Users can view nearby reviews, ratings, and share their own living experiences.

Common Questions

How do I submit a review?
Open the app, choose the relevant location or address, and complete the review form.

Can I request removal of my content?
Yes. Email us with details and we will review your request.

How do I report inaccurate or inappropriate content?
Please contact us with the review details or screenshot.
