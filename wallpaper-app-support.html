Frequently asked questions

  • Why isn’t the wallpaper being applied?

    Please make sure your device’s operating system is up to date. Check the app permissions (storage, photos, etc.). If the issue continues, try closing the app completely and reopening it.

  • How often do you add new wallpapers?

    We try to add new collections regularly. If your app is up to date, new content will automatically appear in the list.

  • I see ads, is this normal?

    We show a limited number of ads to cover development and maintenance costs. We’ve tried to keep them as unobtrusive as possible.
