Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how HomeReview collects, uses, and protects information when you use our app.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as reviews, ratings, addresses or locations you submit, contact details, and support messages.

We may also collect app usage information, device information, approximate location data, crash logs, and analytics data to improve the app.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, show reviews and ratings, improve features, prevent abuse, respond to support requests, and maintain community safety.

4. Location and Address Information

HomeReview may use location or address-related information to show nearby reviews and place user-submitted reviews on the map. Do not submit private or sensitive information you do not want displayed.

5. User-Submitted Content

Reviews, ratings, and related information you submit may be visible to other users. Please avoid sharing personal, sensitive, or confidential information in public reviews.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate, host, analyze, or secure the app, or when required by law.

7. Data Retention

We keep information as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

8. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to legal and technical limitations.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Children

HomeReview is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
