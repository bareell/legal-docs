Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reviva ("we", "our", "us"), a mobile application provided by Barbaros İhtiyar (the "Developer"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. Reviva is an AI-powered photo restoration app that lets you repair, enhance, colorize, and restore your photographs.

By downloading, installing, or using Reviva, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Photos You Upload

The core function of Reviva requires you to upload photos so they can be processed by AI models. When you start a restoration, enhancement, colorization, or full-restore job:

  • Your selected photo is uploaded to our secure cloud storage.
  • The photo is sent to a third-party AI processing provider that runs the model and returns a processed result.
  • The original (input) and the processed (output) images are stored so the app can show your results, before/after comparisons, and let you download or re-access them.
We process your photos solely to provide the restoration features you request. We do not use your photos to train AI models, and we do not use them for advertising or profiling.

1.2. Account & Identifiers

Reviva uses lightweight authentication so your photos and subscription stay tied to your device/account. We may process:

  • An anonymous account identifier and authentication token,
  • A referral code (if you invite friends or redeem an invite),
  • Your email address and message content only if you contact us voluntarily.

1.3. Subscription & Purchase Data

Reviva offers a premium subscription. Payments are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription status (active/expired, plan, expiry date, anonymized purchase identifiers) — we never receive or store your full payment card details.

1.4. Device, Technical & Usage Data

  • Device model and operating system version,
  • App version, language and region settings,
  • Anonymized crash and error logs,
  • Aggregated usage events (e.g. which features are used) to improve the app.

2. Photo Library Permission

Reviva requests access to your photo library so you can select photos to restore and save the results back to your device. The app accesses only the photos you choose; it does not scan or upload your entire gallery in the background.

You can revoke this permission at any time from your device settings, though selecting and saving photos will then be limited.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core features (restore, HD enhance, colorize, full restore),
  • To store and display your input/output images and before/after comparisons,
  • To verify your subscription status and unlock premium features,
  • To enforce fair-use limits and prevent abuse,
  • To diagnose crashes and improve performance and quality,
  • To respond to your support requests,
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

Your uploaded photos and processed results are retained while your account is active so you can re-access them. You may delete individual results in the app. When you request account deletion (or after a prolonged period of inactivity), your stored input and output images and associated account data are deleted from our systems. Anonymized, aggregated logs that no longer identify you may be retained for analytics and security.

6. Data Security & International Transfers

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encrypted transit, access-restricted storage, scoped upload URLs) to protect your data. Because our providers operate globally, your data may be processed in countries other than your own; we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, to object to or restrict processing, or to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Reviva is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version becomes effective as soon as it is published. Continued use of Reviva after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
